Terms and Conditions

The members can use the library for the purpose of study and research. In addition, those who are not the members, can also use the library adhering to the institution's policy. The CCTVs are installed in the library premises. We regret the inconvenience caused to your privacy but the CCTVs are required for security reasons. The applicant needs to fill in all the required details to become the member of the library. (For more information, please see the membership details discussed below.) Kindly deposit your luggage in the locker and obtain the token. The purpose of Meher Library & Jafari Seminary is to help gain knowledge. It is a private institution and retains the right to revoke the membership and also the permission to enter and use the estate if they engage in criminal behaviour and breach of the rules and procedures of the institution.

The membership card is required to issue a book. The books will not be issued in case of failure to carry the membership card. The maximum of 4 books can be issued on one membership card at a time. The book(s) can be issued to the member for the limit of 30 days. The books must be returned to the library before the limit of 30 days. In case of failure to return the issued book within the allowed limit, the member shall have to pay ₹10/- per day per book. An issued book can be renewed only if another member has not reserved the said book. In case of any damage done to the issued book and the usability of the book gets over, the member needs to pay the total price of the book. The visitors who have not obtained the membership of the library can use the library by paying ₹ 50/- per day. Such visitors will not be issued any book.

The e-library service shall be provided to the members wherein he/she can access the books in digitized format. Print, copy, and scan services will be provided to the members.

I agree to abide by the policies of Meher Library & Jafari Seminary and accept responsibility for all the materials and / or service fees charged to my membership. I understand that my failure to abide by the rules and policies of the institute may result in Suspension or revocation of member privileges.
